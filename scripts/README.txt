README.txt

These R scripts are to accompany the e-textbook Risk Analysis in the Earth Sciences: A Lab Manual with Exercises in R, edited by Patrick J. Applegate and Klaus Keller with contributions by Patrick J. Applegate, Ryan L. Sriver, Gregory G. Garner, Richard B. Alley, and Klaus Keller.  

You can open these scripts in a standard text editor, but they are best viewed in RStudio, which you can download for free from https://www.rstudio.com.
